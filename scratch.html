The next game is called Miner tycoon. In this game you can mine stuff, sell it and then buy a new pickaxe to get more ores.

Miner tycoon:

To see the inventory press e

To see the shop to sale ores press f

To clear your inventory to sale the ores press c

To see the shop to buy pickaxes press a

On the pickaxe shop press the button between () to get that pickaxe
